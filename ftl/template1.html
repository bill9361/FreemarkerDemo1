Freemarker Template
获取字符串数据：
data:${hello}
获取对象数据：
PersonId:${person.id}
PersonName:${person.name}
获取List集合数据：
<#list personList as p> PersonId:${p.id} PersonName:${p.name}
获取Map集合数据：
<#list strMap?keys as itemKey> ${itemKey} == ${strMap[itemKey]}
获取List<Map<String,String>>集合数据：
<#list strListMap as strMap> <#list strMap?keys as itemKey> ${itemKey} == ${strMap[itemKey]}
